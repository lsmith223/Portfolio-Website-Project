about me

Hi! My name is Lauren. I am a Junior at the University of Northern Iowa, and I am currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I am 21 years old. You can find my professional information over at my portfolio, but if you'd like to know more about me personally, keep reading!

I was born and raised in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, on the southwest side. After graduating from Prairie High School in May of 2023, I attended Kirkwood Community College and earned my Associates of Science in May of 2025. I plan to graduate with my bachelors in May of 2027.

In Computer Science, I have an interest in Cybersecurity, Web Development, and Operating Systems!

My general interests include: Writing (creative & professional), video games, modding, reading (sci-fi & dystopian genres), animals, arts & crafts, baking, and long documentaries. Definitely not an exhaustive list; most of the time, I'm trying new things depending on what catches my eye.
